Why Leeds?

Leeds has the oldest running commercial railway in the world

Established in 1758, the Middleton Railway holds the distinction of being the world's oldest continuously working railway. It was originally built to transport coal from the Middleton Colliery to the thriving markets of Leeds.

Step into Leeds'fascinating history, filled with the magic and splendor of a bygone era. Explore the industrial past, where the heritage of steam and coal comes alive with every chuff and whistle.

CAFÉS and Tea Rooms

My favourite cafés and Tea rooms in Leeds

Coffee on the Crescent

This delightful spot belongs to ex-professional cricketer Tim Linley and unfolds a captivating tale. Tim's passion for coffee resonates in each carefully brewed cup, yet it transcends beyond coffee; it's a celebration of community. The cafe champions local artisans with a thoughtfully curated array of art and gifts, actively engages in supporting food banks, and spearheads an initiative to revive a neglected section of Woodhouse Moor. These are just a few avenues through which this Leeds cafe makes a positive impact. Come for the philosophy, linger for the exceptional coffee.

Address:

2 The Crescent, Hyde Park, Leeds, LS6 2NW

What I like it about it

A matcha latte is one of my favourite drinks, and they source it directly from Japan; it's incredibly tasty. Additionally, they offer oat milk at the shop. The ice cream is exceptionally creamy and delicious. I have a particular fondness for the brownies, especially the salty caramel flavour!

Betty's Café Tea Rooms

Nestled in the scenic Wharfedale valley, this Café Tea Rooms enjoys a charming location that appeals to both savvy shoppers exploring the town's chic boutiques and outdoor enthusiasts embarking on or returning from a moorland ramble.

Address:

32 The Grv, Ilkley LS29 9EE

What I like about it

I love cakes for their delicious flavours, especially the Lemon Curd Loaf Cake. Macarons are like tiny bursts of joy with their delicate shells and sweet fillings. Afternoon tea is a delightful ritual, sipping on tea, enjoying sandwiches, and indulging in sweet pastries - it's a simple joy that makes any day special. I adore the beautiful tea room ambiance that adds to the charm. It's not just about the delightful treats; it's also the comfort and warmth that make the experience truly special.

FINT

Bringing a Scandinavian twist to the traditional café experience, FINT exudes a light, airy, and laid-back ambiance. Whether you're craving a delightful slice of cake with your coffee, opting for a wholesome brunch, or diving into a satisfying lunch, FINT delivers with its distinctive style - offering dishes that are intriguing and elevated yet always familiar. Treat yourself to the indulgent goodness of chai black rice pudding, adorned with figs, pumpkin seeds, and saffron-infused maple syrup. Complement your meal with a Japanese matcha tea or explore their specialty red velvet lattes for an added touch of luxury. If you're leaning towards a healthier option, give their Green Smoothie a try, brimming with the goodness of spinach, apple, mint, and mango. FINT promises a culinary experience that's both unique and delicious.

Address:

3 Great George St, Leeds LS1 3BR

What I like about it

Just like Seagull Method, the owners are incredibly friendly and chatty. It has the best coffee in Lisbon!
